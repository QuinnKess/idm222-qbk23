About Me

Hi, my name is Quinn Kessler, and I’m a second-year student at Drexel University majoring in User Experience and Interaction Design (UXID). I grew up in Gaithersburg, Maryland, just outside of Washington, D.C., where I first developed a passion for creativity and design through photography, digital projects, and traditional design work.

Since starting at Drexel, I’ve gotten involved in several great campus communities. I’m a proud member of Delta Sigma Phi fraternity, where I’ve built lasting friendships and strong connections. I also play for the Drexel Club Baseball team, which has been an awesome way to stay active and competitive.

I love meeting new people through classes, clubs, and everyday campus life. As I continue my college journey, I’m excited to keep growing, learning, and making the most of every opportunity—both in and out of the classroom.
